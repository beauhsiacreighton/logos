Our Story

Led by Someone Who Has Been Where You Are

Logos was built from a simple observation: the gap between wanting to publish and knowing how to publish is exactly where most medical careers stall.

"I built the exact step-by-step process I wish I had — one that produces real, submittable, publication-ready research without the guesswork."

Having personally guided over 400 pre-medical and medical students to earn more than 500 authorships in peer-reviewed journals, this program exists to give you exactly that process — structured, rigorous, and proven at scale.

As an incoming ENT resident, the founder understands what residency programs look at, what kind of research moves the needle, and exactly what it takes to cross the finish line from zero to published.

The Program

Every Stage. Guided.

01
Topic Selection
We identify a focused, publishable research question tailored to your background and target journals.
02
Literature Search & Screening
You conduct a rigorous, reproducible literature search using PubMed, Google Scholar, and other indexed databases.
03
Data Extraction & Methodology
We walk through the methodology step by step — no prior research experience required.
04
Statistical Interpretation
You receive statistical results and learn to interpret them into precise, publishable scientific language.
05
Manuscript Drafting
Structured writing support through every section — Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion — to exact journal standards.
06
Review & Submission Prep
Your manuscript is refined until it meets requirements for submission to a peer-reviewed, PubMed-indexed journal.

Important Disclosure
Please Read Before Enrolling

Logos Medical Research Institute is a mentorship and research training program — not a publication service. Your enrollment covers structured, step-by-step guidance through the full research process, culminating in a manuscript prepared to meet peer-reviewed journal submission standards.

Publication is not guaranteed. All acceptance decisions are made exclusively by independent journal editors and peer reviewers. We have no influence over, and make no promises regarding, any journal's decision.

We do not pay journals for acceptance. We do not submit on your behalf. We do not guarantee authorship at any specific journal or impact factor.

What we commit to: you will be genuinely mentored, your work will be held to a high standard, and your manuscript will be submission-ready when the program concludes. The research experience and skills you build are yours permanently — regardless of any journal's decision.

This is mentorship. Not a publication package.
